## Why Now? The Timing Is Perfect.

Three forces have converged in 2024–2025 to make this the right moment:

**1. Regulatory Pressure on Payers**
The CMS finalized rules in January 2024 requiring payers to respond to prior auth requests within 7 days (standard) and 72 hours (urgent). This increased volume and urgency for providers to submit faster and track responses more actively.

**2. AI Web Agents Just Became Real**
Until TinyFish and similar infrastructure emerged, no tool could reliably navigate live insurance portals. The tech barrier has just been crossed — and the market hasn't caught up yet. This is a **12–18 month window** before enterprise players notice.

**3. Healthcare Staffing Crisis**
Post-COVID, medical billing staff turnover is at record highs. Practices can't hire fast enough. They are actively looking for automation. The cultural resistance to AI in admin roles has dropped significantly.

### The Key Gap ClearClaim Fills

Every existing solution relies on **EDI/API integrations** with payers. The problem: only ~40% of payer transactions support EDI. The remaining 60% — mostly smaller regional payers and specific workflows — require **manual portal navigation**.

ClearClaim's web agent approach covers 100% of payers. That's the moat.

## Go-To-Market Plan

### Phase 1: Land (Month 1–3) — Get 10 Paying Customers

**Target Customer Profile:**
- Specialty clinic (orthopedic, radiology, oncology, neurology)
- 2–10 physicians
- 100–500 prior auth requests/month
- Has in-house billing staff (not outsourced)
- Located in states with high PA burden (TX, FL, CA, NY, PA)

**Acquisition Channels:**

*Channel 1: Direct Outreach to Office Managers*
Office managers, not physicians, own this pain. LinkedIn outreach + personalized email.
Message: *"We automated prior authorization portal submissions for a radiology clinic in Dallas — they saved 12 hours/week in week one. Want a live demo?"*

*Channel 2: Medical Billing Facebook Groups & Forums*
85,000+ active medical billing professionals in Facebook groups (e.g., "Medical Billing & Coding Network"). Share value-first content — post about denial rate benchmarks, PA tips — build trust before pitching.

*Channel 3: Healthcare Conferences*
MGMA Annual (Oct), HIMSS, AAO (ophthalmology), AOA (orthopedic) — these are where office managers and billing directors gather. One booth can generate 50+ qualified leads.

*Channel 4: Partner with Medical Billing Associations*
AMBA (American Medical Billing Association) and PAHCOM offer member directories and co-marketing. A recommended integration from their newsletter reaches 20,000+ billing professionals.

**Phase 1 Goal:** 10 paying customers at $799/month = **$7,990 MRR** by end of Month 3.

---

### Phase 2: Expand (Month 3–6) — Scale to $50K MRR

**Expand Within Existing Customers**
Each clinic has a billing company they work with, or refers other practices to. Implement a **referral program**: $500 credit per successful referral.

**Target Medical Billing Outsourcing Companies**
There are 5,000+ medical billing companies in the US that manage admin for 10–50 practices each. Landing one billing company = access to their entire client base.

*Value prop for billing companies:* "ClearClaim makes your team 3x more productive. You can take on 3x more clients without hiring more staff."

**Pricing for Billing Companies:** White-label platform option at $4,000–$8,000/month for unlimited client practices.

**EHR Marketplace Listings**
List on Epic App Orchard, athenahealth Marketplace, and DrChrono App Directory. These marketplaces have verified, active practices ready to integrate.

**Phase 2 Goal:** 50–70 customers, mix of direct and billing company accounts. **$50,000 MRR** by end of Month 6.

---

### Phase 3: Dominate (Month 6–12) — Path to $1M ARR

**Product Expansion — Beyond Prior Auth**
- Credentialing automation (physician credentials verified with payers)
- Remittance advice processing (automated ERAs)
- Real-time eligibility at point of scheduling

Each new workflow = upsell opportunity to existing customers.

**Geographic Expansion**
Launch targeted campaigns in the top 5 states by specialty clinic density. Partner with state medical associations for credibility.

**Enterprise Motion**
Begin outbound to regional hospital groups (50–200 physician organizations). These contracts are $50K–$200K ACV and take 3–6 months to close, so plant seeds in Month 6 to close in Month 12.

**Phase 3 Goal:** $1M ARR (~100 customers, mix of tiers). Clear path to $3M ARR with enterprise motion.
